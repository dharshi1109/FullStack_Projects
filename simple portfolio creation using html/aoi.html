Languages Known

English
English is widely recognized as a global lingua franca, serving as a vital medium for international communication and commerce. Its prevalence as a commercial language has facilitated global business interactions and cultural exchange.
Tamil
Tamil is a Dravidian language spoken predominantly in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu and northeastern Sri Lanka, with a rich literary and cultural heritage dating back thousands of years. It holds official status in both regions and has a significant diaspora, contributing to its global presence.
Telugu
Telugu is a Dravidian language primarily spoken in the Indian states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. It has a rich literary history, with classical texts dating back centuries, and serves as the official language in both states. Telugu is also spoken by diaspora communities worldwide, emphasizing its cultural significance beyond the Indian subcontinent.
German
German is a West Germanic language spoken predominantly in Germany, Austria, Switzerland, and Liechtenstein. It is known for its grammatical complexity, compound words, and influence in various fields such as science, philosophy, and literature. Additionally, it is one of the most widely spoken languages in the European Union.
